How to Play

  • Enter your name on the home page and select a quiz category to begin.
  • Each quiz consists of multiple-choice questions related to football.
  • You have a total of 3 minutes to answer as many questions as you can.
  • Select the correct answer from the given options to score points.
  • Your high score will be saved, and you can try to beat it in future attempts.
